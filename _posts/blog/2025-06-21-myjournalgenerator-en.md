I released [MyJournalGenerator](/myjournalgenerator/dist/), an AI-powered web app for creating personalized journals based on philosophical traditions, life roles and central objectives.

It can generate a custom index, daily and weekly entries, reflections, prompts, challenges, covers and illustrations. It also supports EPUB export, local persistence and import/export backup.

The idea is simple: instead of a generic journal template, build a structure that fits the user's chosen traditions and current life problem.

MyJournalGenerator is built with React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS and the Google Gemini API.
