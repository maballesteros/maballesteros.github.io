# Ebook Specifications: Diario del Guerrero

> **Standard**: Platinum Quality (Diario)

## Word Count
*   **Daily Entry**: 500 - 700 words.
*   **Monthly Intro**: 1000+ words.

## Tone
*   Stoic, disciplined, reflective.
*   Second person ("You").
*   No "fluff". Direct and actionable.

## Formatting
*   Use blockquotes for key quotes.
*   Headers should be H2 for daily titles.
