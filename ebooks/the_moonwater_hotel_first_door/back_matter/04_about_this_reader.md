# About This Reader

*The Moonwater Hotel: The First Door* is an A2+/B1 English reader for families.

It is designed for shared reading, not silent study. The story gives readers repeated useful language inside a mystery adventure, with short support sections to help conversation.

- Chapters 2-10 are structured as "Coming soon" pages with canon outlines.
- The final support sections are ready as a base.

The next editorial step is to write chapters 2-10 in full, using chapter 1 as the style and continuity canon.
