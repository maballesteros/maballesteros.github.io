## Coming soon

This chapter is planned but not written yet.

Nora, Sam and Aunt Iris must answer the Guest Book before the tide returns. Mr Vale explains only part of the truth: the Moonwater Hotel does not force anyone, but it does invite certain guests to become guardians.

Aunt Iris wants to take the children back to the hotel immediately. Nora studies the words carefully and begins to understand that the choice is not random. Sam suggests **sky**, because it sounds safer than **sea**. Morrow, the black cat, touches the word **sand** with his white paw.

The family chooses **sand**.

The blue door opens into an underground chamber called **The Sand Room**.

### Cliffhanger

Inside the Sand Room, they find recent footprints, although nobody should have been there in years.
