Mr Vale explains three important rules:

1. Never enter a door when the tide is coming in.
2. Never take what the room has not given you.
3. Never make a promise to the sea unless you mean it.

Sam almost breaks one rule when he touches the compass, but Morrow stops him. Aunt Iris demands the truth, and Mr Vale reveals that the Bell family has been connected to the Moonwater Hotel before.

The Sand Room begins to fill with sand.

### Cliffhanger
