# Ebook Specifications: The Moonwater Hotel: The First Door

## Project Goal

Build a family English reader titled *The Moonwater Hotel: The First Door*.

The book is an A2+/B1 soft English reading adventure for Spanish-speaking families. It should feel like a simple young-adult mystery novel, not a workbook and not a children's picture book.

## Language

- Main story: English.
- Support sections: English with Spanish help where useful.
- Level: A2+/B1 soft.
- Tone: coastal mystery, family adventure, gentle suspense, humor, warmth.

## Canon

Chapter 1, "The Door Beneath the Tide", is provided by the user and must not be regenerated. It is the absolute canon for:

- Nora, Sam, Aunt Iris, Mr Vale and Morrow.
- The Moonwater Hotel.
- The blue door beneath the tide.
- The Guest Book.
- The choice: sand, sea or sky.
- Narrative level, rhythm, humor and cliffhanger style.

## Chapter Format

Each chapter should follow this structure:

```markdown
# Chapter X - Title

## Helpful words

| English | Espanol |
| ------- | ------- |

[Story in English]

## Easy comprehension questions

1. ...

## Useful expressions

* **Expression.** -> Traduccion.

## Talk together

* ...
```

## Full Book Structure

1. Title page.
2. Copyright / usage note.
3. Foreword.
4. How to use this book.
5. Main characters.
6. Chapters 1-10.
7. Final recap.
8. Vocabulary bank.
9. Family reading challenges.
10. About this reader.

## Quality Bar

- English must be natural, accessible and not childish.
- Dialogue should carry humor and character.
- Do not explain all mysteries too early.
- Mr Vale remains ambiguous.
- Morrow should not talk in the initial setup.
- Aunt Iris must be active and have her own arc.
- The book resolves the first door while leaving the second door open.
